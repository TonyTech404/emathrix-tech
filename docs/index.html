FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our technologies and solutions

What is ArchVision and how does it work?

ArchVision is our AI-powered infrastructure inspection system that uses drones and AGVs equipped with cameras and LiDAR sensors to scan infrastructure like buildings, bridges, and roads. The collected data is processed by our AI algorithms to detect defects like cracks or corrosion, and then visualized in 3D models for easy identification and assessment.

What types of infrastructure can ArchVision inspect?

ArchVision is designed to inspect various types of infrastructure including buildings, bridges, dams, tunnels, roads, and railway systems. The system is adaptable to different environments and can be configured to detect specific types of defects depending on the infrastructure being inspected.

When will your educational platform be available?

Our educational platform for connecting students with tutors is currently in development, with a planned beta release in the coming months. We'll be announcing more details and opportunities for early access on our website and social media channels.

What age groups are your robotics education programs designed for?

Our robotics education programs are being designed for children aged 8-16, with different modules adapted to various age groups and skill levels. The programs will teach kids the basics of robotics, programming, and AI through fun, hands-on projects and challenges.

Are you looking for investment or partnership opportunities?

As a growing startup, we're open to discussing investment opportunities and strategic partnerships that align with our vision. We're particularly interested in collaborations that can help us accelerate our product development, expand our market reach, or enhance our technological capabilities.

How can I stay updated on your products and developments?

You can follow our progress by subscribing to our newsletter, following us on social media, or checking back on our website regularly. We post updates on our product development, upcoming releases, and any events or presentations we're participating in.
